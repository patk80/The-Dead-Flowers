About

New Brunswick, NJ based 4 piece Rock 'n Roll band formed in 2015.

The Dead Flowers provide a high energy, Rock 'n Roll show that brings involuntary movement of the head and hips, that proves time and time again that Rock 'n Roll is alive and well.
